Our Heritage.
Our Science.

Since 2008, Antique Protocol has merged the traditional connoisseurship of cartography with cutting-edge forensic science to protect the heritage of our history's most precious maps.

The Forensic Laboratory.

Located in the heart of Mayfair, our facility is a dedicated sanctuary for historical investigation. Every map entering our care undergoes a systematic audit process that is unrivaled in the private sector.

We serve world-class museums, corporate archives, and the most discerning private collectors, ensuring that every acquisition is a secure investment in human history.

Our Core Principles

Absolute Integrity

We maintain an uncompromising commitment to the truth of provenance. If a document's history is clouded, we reveal it without hesitation.

Scientific Precision

Using multi-spectral imaging and chemical analysis, we provide forensic certainty where others offer only opinions.

Global Stewardship

We view ourselves as temporary guardians of humanity's geographic heritage, ensuring its survival for generations.

Global Command.

Our network extends from the archival vaults of Europe to the private galleries of Tokyo and the tech hubs of Silicon Valley.
