Our Story

Preserving history on paper for over four decades.

Four Decades of Excellence

Founded in 1985 in the heart of London, Antique Map & Print Dealer has been a trusted companion for collectors and museums worldwide.

We are dedicated to the preservation of rare cartographic materials and fine prints. Our curators specialize in everything from early Dutch mapmaking golden age to Victorian industrial city plans.

  • Expert authentication services
  • Museum-grade restoration lab
  • Custom archival framing solutions
  • Global sourcing for rare specific requests

The Cartographer's Promise

A map is more than lines on paper—it's a window into the dreams and discoveries of our ancestors.

Integrity

We guarantee the authenticity and provenance of every piece in our collection.

Quality

Each piece is professionally preserved using acid-free archival techniques.

Community
